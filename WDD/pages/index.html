We are Kirkstead Farm, a farm situated near the Kirkstead Burn, near St Mary's loch in the Scottish Borders (about halfway between Edinburgh and Carlisle). We feature the following activities:

  • Guided walks and signposted paths around the local area
  • A play park
  • Our farm shop, selling local produce and plants grown by us and our neighbours
  • A wee petting zoo, where you can meet our beloved friendly animals
